About Eliot Carlsen

Background

I grew up in Sonoma, California, better known as Wine Country because of the many wineries in the area. When I was growing up Sonoma was a small agricultural town. Now it attacts thousands of tourists every year.

After high school, I bounced around to a few different cities including, San Francisco, Santa Cruz and Seattle. Eventually, I was accepted into UC San Diego where I got my degree in Biological Anthropology. I then moved back up to San Francisco where I got a job in a climbing gym. While there I moved my way up in the climbing gym industry and eventually started managing.

During this time I was always interested in the tech industry. Many of my friends were accomplished programmers and developers. Eventually, I decided to take a class about Python and really loved it. It was then that I decided I wanted to learn programming more in depth.
