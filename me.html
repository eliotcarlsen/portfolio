About the Developer

Background

I grew up in a small town called Sonoma, California. It's better known as Wine Country. For me, it was a small agricultural town. After graduating from High School I moved to a few different cities attending Junior Colleges. Eventually, I was accepted into UC San Diego where I got my degree in Biological Anthropology. I then moved back up to the Bay Area where I got a job in a climbing gym. I was working in San Francisco and moved my way up in the gym industry eventually Managing two large gyms in Sunnyvale and SF.

During this time I was always interested in the other industries in the area. And a few of my friends were accomplished programmers. I decided to take a few classes about the subject and really loved it. I eventually decided that I wanted to learn more in depth so applied for bootcamps.
